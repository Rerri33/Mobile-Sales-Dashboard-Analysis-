# Mobile Sales Dashboard Analysis (Power BI)

##   Project Overview  
This project analyzes mobile device sales data to uncover **revenue drivers, customer behavior, and sales performance** across regions and channels.

The dashboard was built using Microsoft Power BI to transform raw data into actionable business insights.

## 🎯 Business Objective  
The goal of this analysis was to:
- Understand how revenue flows into the company  
- Identify top-performing products and brands  
- Analyze customer demographics and behavior  
- Evaluate sales channels and payment preferences  

---

## 📈 Key Insights  

### 🔹 Sales Performance  
- OnePlus recorded the highest units sold (**4.4K**)  
- Apple generated the highest revenue (**$31.6M**)  
→ Highlights the difference between high-volume and high-value brands  

---

### 💰 Revenue Channels  
- Online sales contribute **62.32%** of total revenue  
- Retail stores contribute **12.05%**  
→ Indicates strong digital adoption and reliance on online channels  

---

### 💳 Payment Behavior  
- EMI and Credit Card are the most used payment methods  
- EMI is the most preferred option overall  

---

### 👥 Customer Insights  
- Age group **26–33** generated the highest revenue (**$31.1M**)  
- Age group **50–57** contributed the least (**$1.7M**)  
- Females aged **26–44** are the most active buyers  

---

### 🌍 Regional Insights  
- India and Europe show similar pricing and value patterns  

---

### 📉 Trends  
- Retail sales show a declining trend  
- Online channels continue to dominate  

---
